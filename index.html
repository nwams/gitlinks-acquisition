GitLinks is now part of the Infor family. Thank you to all of our supporters who made it possible.

GitLinks community and friends,

We’re excited to announce that GitLinks was acquired by Infor a multibillion-dollar enterprise software provider and strategic business partner for more than 90,000 organizations worldwide. With Infor’s 168 offices and 16,000+ employees around the world we’re glad that our product could provide value to such a global organization.

Since we started three years ago as graduate students at Cornell Tech we’ve been on a mission to bring open source securely into the enterprise environment, and we built amazing software to automate application security and license compliance.

We’ve discovered an exciting opportunity to apply our team’s strength in open source infrastructure and enterprise software development to help Infor’s clients be more successful.

We want to thank you - our loyal users, mentors, and investors. You supported and trusted us and are the reason we have this incredible new opportunity ahead of us.

Thank you for being a part of our journey!
